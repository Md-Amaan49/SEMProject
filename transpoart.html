Modes of Transportation

  • Road Transport: Ideal for short to medium distances within the country.
  • Rail Transport: Cost-effective bulk movement across regions.
  • Air Freight: For high-value or time-sensitive produce.
  • Sea Freight: Best for bulk export and long-distance trade.

Packaging Considerations

  • Ensure packaging suits the transport mode and weather conditions.
  • Label packages with handling instructions and destination clearly.
  • Use pallets for bulk loads to enable faster loading/unloading.
