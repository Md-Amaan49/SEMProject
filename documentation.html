Essential Export Documents

  • Invoice: A detailed invoice listing the product, price, and shipping terms.
  • Export License: Required in many countries for legal exportation of certain goods.
  • Certificate of Origin: A document that verifies where the product was produced.
  • Bill of Lading: A contract between the shipper and the carrier, providing proof of shipment.
  • Health and Safety Certificates: Ensures that the products meet the importing country's safety regulations.
