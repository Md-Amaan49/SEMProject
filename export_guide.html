Step-by-Step Export Process

  1. Register your business for international trade.
  2. Identify target markets and their import regulations.
  3. Obtain required certifications (e.g., organic, phytosanitary).
  4. Prepare compliant packaging and labeling.
  5. Choose suitable transportation and logistics partners.
  6. Complete all documentation (invoice, packing list, bill of lading).
  7. Get your products tested and cleared by inspection agencies.
  8. Ship and track the consignment to the destination.

Tips for First-Time Exporters

  • Start with a small, manageable market.
  • Understand Incoterms and international payment terms.
  • Use professional help for documentation and compliance.
  • Maintain quality and consistency to build reputation.
  • Stay updated on changes in import/export regulations.
